~-PILLARS OF ISLAM-~

What Hold Islam So Strong



1. Shahadah [Faith]

Believing that there is no god but Allah, and Muhammad (ﷺ) is His Messenger. It is the foundation of Islam.

2. Salah [Prayer]

Performing the five daily prayers to worship Allah, seek guidance, and strengthen faith.

3. Zakat [Charity]

Giving a portion of wealth to those in need, promoting compassion, justice, and community support.

4. Sawm [Fasting]

Fasting during the month of Ramadan from dawn to sunset to develop self-control and gratitude.

5. Hajj [Pilgrimage]

Making the pilgrimage to Makkah at least once in a lifetime if physically and financially able.
