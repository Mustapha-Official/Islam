About This Site & Me


Al-salamu alaukum wa rahmatu'llahi wa barakaatuhu!

You can call me Mustapha Gbanie. I built this website only for the sake of Allah—my wish is to share clear, simple and correct knowledge about Islam, so everyone can easily understand our beautiful faith, learn about Allah, know the Five Pillars, and find helpful resources as Allah Azzawajal has willed it.

I am just a servant of Allah Almighty, still learning every day just like all of us. All information here is based on the Qur'an and the authentic Sunnah of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ. If you ever see any mistake or something that needs correction, please kindly tell me through the contact details in the footer—your help is truly valued, and we are all here to seek the truth together.

All that is right comes only from Allah; any shortcoming or error is from me alone.

Also, I encourage you to read the two first questions and answers of my FAQ page for clear assurance.

May Allah accept this small effort from me, make it beneficial for every person who visits, guide us all to what pleases Him, and keep us firm upon the straight path. Allahummah Ameen
